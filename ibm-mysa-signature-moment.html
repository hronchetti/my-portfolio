Designing an innovative way to share a success story across a multi-national corporation.

      IBM My Sales Activity (MySA) is a new tool allowing IBM employees and IBM Business Partners to track and manage their sales opportunities with clients effectively. The tool itself was designed to replace a legacy system that experienced numerous problems and was very costly to maintain.
      ‘IBM MySA Signature Moment’ was a milestone for the solution that marked 1,000 user sign-ups. My team was tasked with creating an innovative way to share this success across the whole company, our solution: a professionally designed keynote accompanied by an interactive one page website.

Vibrant Colour Pallet

The success of MySA needed to be enticing, in order to encourage users to look further into the project I used colour psychology to my advantage. Combining purple, white and green allowed me to create a vibrant/exciting environment that conveyed growth, ambition and success.
