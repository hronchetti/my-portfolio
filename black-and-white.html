Capturing elegance, choreographing art and controlling perspective.

      In my spare time I organise and take part in semi-professional photography shoots. Typically, I create an artistic concept; advertise it to upcoming models, make-up artists and professional hair stylists, then carry out a photography shoot on location or in a studio.
      In May 2015, I created a concept based around captivating black and white imagery. The shoot took place in Centre Studio, Newcastle Upon-Tyne, UK and involved alluring low light scenarios that focused on expressing emotion and showing elegance.

Drawing focus

To create captivating images I edited with contrast in mind. Using various techniques I emphasised the model's eyes, lips and hair to produce piercing scenes. On some occasions I added extra elements to enhance focus and allure viewers further.
