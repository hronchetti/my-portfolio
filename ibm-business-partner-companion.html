Revolutionising the way Business Partners connect with IBM through providing a customised user experience.

      In the past, Business Partners had to use a large combination of services to stay up to date with IBM news and events. Because of this, the reach on many communication streams was lower than desired.
      IBM Business Partner Companion is a mobile app devoted to giving users instant updates related to upcoming conferences and events. Features include: a feed for upcoming events, ‘your events’ facility to track subscribed events, notifications service to keep users up to date and a profile area to update user preferences.

Building trust

To add value we integrated users’ IBM PartnerWorld IDs into the solution so onboarding was fast. This, combined with colour theory research helped us reassure users that IBM Business Partner Companion was a trustworthy application that could be relied upon to offer up to date (and accurate) information.
