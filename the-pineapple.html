Creating a brand, capturing authenticity and stimulating growth for a family business.

The Pineapple is a traditional Irish pub based in Lambeth, London. Over the last 30 years, the family business has built a loyal customer base of locals and public figures wanting to escape the busy city.

In summer 2016, I began working with the O’Neills to define a brand, expand their online presence and attract more customers.

Together, we created a set of goals the website should achieve, these included: attract more tourists, promote the food menu and encourage more event bookings. All branding work, logo, menu and website design was done by myself and developed manually without a Content Management System.

Authentic visual identity

To reflect the traditional interior of the pub I used rich/earthy colours for The Pineapple’s branding. Font choices followed a classic chalkboard style featured within the pub, and all photography was edited using warm filters to give a snug/cosy feel.
